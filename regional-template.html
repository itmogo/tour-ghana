Tour Ghana

Ahafo Region

The Ahafo Region is a newly created Region in Ghana with Goaso as its capital.

Ashanti Region

The Ashanti Region is known for its major gold bar and cocoa production.

Bono East Region

The new region was carved out of Brong Ahafo region. The capital of the new region is Techiman.

Bono Region

Sunyani is a city and the capital town of the Bono Region and the Sunyani Municipal of Ghana.

Central Region

The Central region attains many tourist attractions such as castles, forts and beaches.

Eastern Region

The capital town of Eastern Region is Koforidua.The Eastern region is the location of the Akosombo dam.

Greater Accra

The capital city of Greater Accra Region is Accra which is at the same time the capital city of Ghana.

Northern Region

The Northern Region is one of the sixteen regions of Ghana. It is located in the north of the country.

Savannah Region

The Savannah Region is one of the newest regions of Ghana and yet the largest region in the country.

North East Region

The North East Region is one of the sixteen regions of Ghana. It was created in December 2018.

Upper East Region

The Upper East regional capital is Bolgatanga, referred to as Bolga. Other towns include Navrongo.

Upper West Region

The Upper West Region of Ghana is located in the north-western corner of Ghana.

Volta Region

Volta Region is one of Ghana's sixteen administrative regions, with Ho designated as its capital.

Oti Region

The Oti Region is one of the six regions created regions of Ghana in December 2018.

Western Region

The Western Region enjoys a long coastline that stretches from South Ghana's border.

Western North Region

The Western North Region has the highest rainfall in Ghana, lush green hills, and fertile soils.
