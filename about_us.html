About Us

Welcome to Tour Ghana Website


Ghana is the most stable country on planet earth. We are a people dearly loved by God and blessed with so much natural resources.

Our Weather conditions is so wonderful - rainly and dry seasons - the pattern has never changed. Farmers and all who depend on it to undertake activities have never been disappointed.

We have diverse kinds of cultral and traditions that are worth experiencing - from the north to the south, from west to the east. We have diverse kinds of dialects, festivals and events across the year to engage people celebrate and fellowship together as one people.

Places of interest to tourist includes forts and castles built by our colonial masters spread across the coastline; animal reserves - elephants, with crocrodile ponds, ranges of birds and monkeys, to different kinds of animal habitats; waterfalls across the country attracting various visitors during occasions and ordinary days all year round.

We have very attractive beaches and resorts for your relaxation on holidays or vacation trips. Our restaurants and hotels serves some of the best local and continental dishes that are tasty with african touch.

Most importantly the Ghanaian is peaceful and accomodates visitors freely. We are warm, loving and good people.

This and many more are what you are going to find browsing our site. We are here to educate and assit you make informed decisions as to places to visit, guide you as you aspire to tour Ghana. Our site is full of all the information to let your stay a pleasant one and worth remembering to visit next time with family and friends.

Incase you need special information you could not find on this site, do not hesitate to contact us immeditely. Once again you are Welcome to Ghana - Gold Coast, in West Africa.
